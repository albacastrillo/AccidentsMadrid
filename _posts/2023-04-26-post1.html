Are you ready to take a deep dive into the world of data analysis and visualisation? We certainly are! As a group of three curious and driven Spanish students, we've taken on an exciting project - analysing and studying a comprehensive dataset containing information on traffic accidents in Madrid.

But why traffic accidents, you might ask? Well, we believe that road safety is a crucial issue that affects us all. By understanding the underlying factors that contribute to traffic accidents, we can work towards creating a safer and more secure environment for all road users.

Our dataset includes a wealth of information, including the age and gender of the victim, the severity of the accident, the time and date of the accident, the weather conditions at the time of the accident, the exact location of the accident, and the type of vehicle involved. With so much valuable information at our fingertips, we're excited to get to work and see what insights we can uncover.

As we dive into our analysis, we're hoping to gain valuable insights into the patterns and trends that underlie traffic accidents in Madrid. By visualising this data in innovative and engaging ways, we're looking to make our findings accessible and informative for a wide range of audiences.

Ultimately, we believe that our project has the potential to make a real difference in the world of road safety. By contributing to the important conversation around traffic accidents and working towards a safer, more secure future for all road users, we hope to make a lasting impact. So, buckle up and get ready for the ride - we can't wait to share our findings with you!
